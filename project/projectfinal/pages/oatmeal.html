Apple Cinnamon Oatmeal

Ingredients

  • ¼ of an Apple
  • ½ cup of rolled oats
  • Water
  • 1 – 1 ½ tsp of cinnamon (to taste)
  • Peanut butter (as topping)
  • Favorite milk (I use oat)

Instructions

  1. Cut the ¼ of the apple into cubes/small pieces
  2. Put the rolled oats in a microwave-safe bowl
  3. Add the apples and cinnamon to the bowl
  4. Fill bowl with water until just above the oats
  5. Place in microwave for 2 minutes, then stir
  6. If more time is needed, use 30 second increments until apples are soft and oats are cooked
  7. Put a spoonful of peanut butter on top of the oats, fill bowl with as much milk as you like
  8. I like the warm/cold combo so I add enough milk to make it similar to cereal
  9. ENJOY!
