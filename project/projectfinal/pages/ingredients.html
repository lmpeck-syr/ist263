Ingredients

Here is a list of key ingredients that you should always have on hand. The colored items have a photo example!

  • Baking Supplies
    • All-purpose flour
    • Granulated sugar
    • Baking soda
    • Baking powder
    • Brown sugar
  • Extra virgin olive oil (EVOO)
  • Vegetable oil
  • Apple cider vinegar
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Honey
  • Rice (white or brown)
  • Pasta (any shape you like)
  • Chicken broth
  • Beans
    • Whatever you like, I always have chickpeas and black beans
  • Peanut butter (sub any alternative if allergic)
  • Milk
  • Unsalted butter
  • Eggs
  • Jelly/jam
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Mustard
  • Hot sauce (my favorite is sriracha)
  • Soy sauce
  • Pickles
  • Frozen fruit
  • Frozen meat (buy fresh, put in freezer until ready to use)
