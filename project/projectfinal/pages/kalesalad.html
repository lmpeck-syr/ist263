Cherry Kale Salad

Ingredients

Dressing:

  • Juice form half of a Lemon
  • 2 Tbs of EVOO
  • 1 tsp of Dijon mustard
  • 1 ½ tsp of apple cider vinegar
  • 1 Tbs of honey
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Salad:

  • 4 leaves of Dino/Lacinato Kale (flat not curly)
  • ½ cup of quartered, pitted cherries
  • ¼ of a cucumber
  • 1 Tbs of EVOO
  • Dressing
  • Parmesan (freshly grated)

Instructions

  1. Combine all dressing ingredients in a small bowl and mix
  2. Adjust the measurements to your own taste
  3. Remove stems from kale
  4. Cut kale into small ribbons (thinner slices are easier to digest)
  5. Place kale in a large bowl, massage with 1 Tbs of olive oil until kale begins to wilt (again, this makes it easier to digest)
  6. Cut cucumber into bite sized pieces
  7. Add cucumber, cherries, and dressing to bowl with the kale and toss the salad until everything is coated
  8. Grilled chicken would also be nice to add if you want to add a protein
  9. Grate parmesan on top of salad to your liking
  10. ENJOY!
