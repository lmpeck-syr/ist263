Sweet Potato Fries

Ingredients

  • 1 sweet potato
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • EVOO

Instructions

  1. Cut the sweet potato into thin fry shapes
  2. In a large bowl, coat sweet potatoes in 2 Tbs of olive oil, salt, and pepper
  3. Cook the fries in the air fryer at about 400 degrees for 15-20 minutes or until crispy
  4. ENJOY!
