Caprese Panini

Ingredients

  • 2 Slices of Sourdough bread
  • Â½ of a Tomato
  • Pesto
  • 3 slices of Mozzarella
  • Mixed greens
  • Balsamic glaze
  • EVOO

Instructions

  1. Heat 1 tbs of EVOO in a skillet over medium high heat
  2. Spread pesto on both sides of the bread
  3. Place mixed greens on one side
  4. On top of that, put slices of tomato, then mozzarella, then drizzle with balsamic glaze
  5. Place the other piece of bread on top
  6. Place the sandwich into preheated skillet
  7. Use a spatula to press sandwich down
  8. Place a lid on top of the pan to help cheese melt
  9. After 3-5 minutes, check to see if sandwich is golden brown and that cheese is slightly melted
  10. If ready, flip sandwich until it is golden brown on the other side
  11. Once ready, cut in half and ENJOY!
