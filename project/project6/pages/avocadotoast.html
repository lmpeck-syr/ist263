Avocado Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 English Muffin
  • ½ of an Avocado
  • EVOO
  • 2 Eggs
  • Everything but the Bagel Seasoning
  • Sriracha

Instructions

  1. Toast the English muffin to your liking
  2. While it’s toasting, heat extra virgin olive oil in a small or medium skillet on medium heat
  3. Once heated, crack 2 eggs in the pan (you should hear them start to sizzle)
  4. Adjust heat if cooking too quickly
  5. Once all whites look cooked, and you can see crispy edges (about 5 mins), flip the egg and cook for another 1-2 minutes
  6. Turn off heat once cooked
  7. Split the half of the avocado between the 2 sides of the English muffin and mash
  8. Sprinkle Everything but the Bagel seasoning on each side
  9. Place the 1 egg on each of the English muffin halves
  10. Drizzle sriracha on top of each side
  11. ENJOY!
