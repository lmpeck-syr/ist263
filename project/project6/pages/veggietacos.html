Vegetable Tacos

Ingredients

  • 1 red bell pepper
  • ½ of a red onion
  • EVOO
  • 1 Tbs of cumin
  • 1 tsp of smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp of chili powder
  • ½ tsp of cayenne pepper
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 3 corn tortillas
  • Spinach
  • ½ of an avocado

Instructions

  1. Preheat a skillet and 2 Tbs of EVOO on medium heat
  2. Slice the bell pepper and red onion into thin pieces
  3. Add vegetables to the skillet with spices
  4. Mix all of the vegetables and spices in pan
  5. Cook for about 8-10 minutes until the vegetables are soft
  6. Mix about every 2 minutes
  7. Heat the corn tortillas in the microwave for about 10-15 seconds
  8. Add spinach to each tortilla
  9. Add vegetables to tacos
  10. Top each taco with slices of avocado
  11. ENJOY!
