Protein Berry Smoothie

Ingredients

  • ½ cup spinach
  • ½ of a frozen banana
  • 1 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 1 Tbs of chia seeds
  • Large scoop of peanut butter
  • Optional – vanilla protein powder
  • Milk of choice (I use oat)

Instructions

  1. Place all ingredients in the blender
    • Use enough milk so that it blends smoothly (about to the top of where the ingredients sit in the blender)
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. ENJOY!
