Lily Peck's Biography

I am a dual major in Television/Radio/Film in Newhouse and Information Management and Technology with a concentration in data analytics in the iSchool. I decided to be a dual major because I wanted to be able to use data analytics skills in the entertainment industry.

This past summer, I interned at HBO on the Content Strategy team. My tasks mainly focused on competitive analysis for the 6 major streamers. This was a great experience for me as it taught me a lot about the entertainment industry first-hand, not simply through a textbook. At the time, the company was going through a huge merger, so it was super beneficial to see how a company transitions through something like that. At Syracuse University, I have been part of the honors program since freshman year and I am a peer mentor for the first-year students. My strongest skills are time management, organization, attention to detail, and independence.
